    Adam Sakari Espinet

    is the fancy man descendant of an ancient line of Viking warriors and swashbuckling pirates from the Caribbean. He is currently settled in Milton, Ontario where he practices freelance web-design and photography. Using his spiritually endowed imagination bestowed upon him by the Norse gods and his unconventional thinking inherited from his pirate ancestors, he creates fantastically emotional works of art and design. He also possesses an uncanny attention to detail in which he uses to help him make good designs even better. This man is strong believer in self-teaching because there is always something to learn and only you can push yourself to new level. To keep his mind sharp he loves to read, digitally paint, sketch, and take awesome photographs. While his love of rock-climbing keeps is body in peak physical condition ready to pillage at any time by land or sea.
